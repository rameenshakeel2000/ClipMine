Features

Meaningful Sharing

ClipMine works great for sharing and embedding recorded video-game sessions, product reviews, talk shows, speeches, lectures and sport coverage. Try out more samples.

Interactive Embeds

Want to enable the ClipMine experience in your Blog or Twitter feed?Just embed or Content-Aware Video Player. It is responsive ad built to work across different modern browers and devices. Start by tagging a video of your choice or pick and already tagged one and simply paste the embed code into your website.

Smart Tagging

Found and interesting point in the video? On ClipMine, adding tags is not reserved for the video owerners only - and community member can do it. In addition or smart algorithm can automatically suggest tags (in private beta) for a wide variety of videos
